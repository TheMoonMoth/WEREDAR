About the Were.com Team

Here at Were.com we are committed to protecting your community from the lycan curse


For thousands of years werewolves have feasted on unsuspecting humans every full moon, but no longer. With the help of Were.com you can now stay safe by tracking werewolf sightings in your neighborhood. This easy to use app includes a map with pins depicting the last known location of every known werewolf in the area, a lunar cycle tracker, and a form to report a sighting of a known or unkown werewolf. These are the essential tools you need to stay alert and stay alive.
